The Bucknell Elysian Project

The mission of the Elysian Bucknell Project is to create a platform for learning new and exciting things about ourselves and the world around us, to encourage innovation and creative imagination to improve, inspire and expand human thinking across the broadest possible horizons. Through the experience of exciting and amazing interactive entertainment that tantalizes the mind and stimulates the imagination, we want to cultivate a generation of Bucknellians with a focus on enlightening and enhancing the community.
